Profile

Joel Fuentes

I am an Assistant Professor at Universidad del Bio-Bio, Chile. I previously worked at Intel and University of California, Irvine.
My research interests are parallel computing, accelerators and compilers.
My CV (last update Feb/2022).

Software Development

I love programming. I develop computer programs in C++, Java and DPC++. There is a set of projects in my repository on Github.

Research

My background is mainly focused on High-Performance Computing (HPC), particularly Multi-core/GPU programming and compilers. In my PhD dissertation I proposed new concurrent data structures for CPU/GPU .

Teaching

I teach computer science classes at Universidad del Bio-Bio. At UC Irvine, I've been TA of some undergraduate and graduate classes.

Hobbies

My hobbies are skiing, photography and soccer.
